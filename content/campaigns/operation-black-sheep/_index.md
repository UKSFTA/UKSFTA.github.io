Militia forces backed up by Russia have seized the border with Estonia and Latvia near the area of Ruha. They have shot down multiple drones and a cargo plane owned by NATO Forces. Due to the AA threat and the unknown situation HQ have ordered UKSF Taskforce Alpha do go deep into the territory to scout out the current situation.
