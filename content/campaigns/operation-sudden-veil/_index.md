ORDERS: OP SUDDEN VEIL
DTG: 251900Z FEB 26
AO: ZAGORSK (Sector East)
TASK ORG: RANGER ELM / SF / SPECIALIST ATTACHMENTS (NON-ATTRIBUTED)

SITUATION
Enemy: Adversary forces have fortified Zagorsk, using the urban center as a primary logistics hub and C2 node. Expect high-density Electronic Warfare (EW), heavy armor in the outskirts, and entrenched infantry in the city core.
Friendly: UAF elements are prepping for a multi-axis assault. We are the "force multiplier" inserted to tip the balance.

MISSION
Task Force elements are to conduct a covert insertion into the Zagorsk AO NLT 251900Z FEB to neutralise key adversary nodes and support UAF in the recapture of the city IOT (in order to) collapse adversary GLOCs.

EXECUTION
 * Concept of Op: A three-phase, low-signature strike.
   * Phase 1 (Infil): Specialized elements and SF insert via black-out terrestrial routes. Establish eyes-on key C2 and EW targets.
   * Phase 2 (The Hit): H-Hour. SF/Specialists hard-hit C2 infrastructure. Rangers provide technical fire support and anti-armor overmatch for UAF lead-ins.
   * Phase 3 (Consolidation): Seize city center. Hand over to UAF. Sterilize AO and exfiltrate.
 * Main Effort: Neutralization of the Zagorsk EW array to restore UAF comms.

COORDINATING INSTRUCTIONS
 * Uniform: STERILE. No Rank, No Name, No UK/MOD Insignia. "Deny and Discard" protocols in effect for all personal kit.
 * ROE: High-intensity. PID (Positive Identification) for combatants. Zero-tolerance for compromise during Infil.
 * Timings: Insertion commences 251900Z.
