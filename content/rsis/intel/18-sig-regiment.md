18 (UKSF) Signal Regiment is a composite regiment of the Royal Corps of Signals, providing specialist communications and SIGINT support to the SAS, SBS, and SRR.

## Core Roles
- **Signal Intelligence (SIGINT):** Interception and analysis of electronic transmissions.
- **Electronic Warfare (EW):** Disrupting adversarial communications and radar systems.
- **Special Forces Communicator (SFC):** Embedding specialist signallers within operational troops.
