The Special Boat Service (SBS) is the UK's principal maritime special forces unit. Comprised primarily of Royal Marines, the SBS specialises in covert operations in the maritime, littoral, and riverine environments.

## Core Operational Domains

### Maritime Counter-Terrorism (MCT)
Protection of strategic maritime assets, including oil platforms, shipping, and port infrastructure. SBS operators are experts in Visit, Board, Search, and Seizure (VBSS) operations.

### Amphibious Reconnaissance
Covert hydrographic surveys and beach reconnaissance to facilitate large-scale landings. The SBS operates ahead of the main force to secure beachheads and gather critical intelligence.

### Underwater Demolition
Specialized sabotage of maritime infrastructure and vessels using advanced diving and demolition techniques.

## Entry & Progression

The Special Boat Service is a **Tier 1** asset. Direct entry is not possible.

* **Selection**: Candidates must first serve in a support role or Tier 2 unit (ASOB/JSFAW) and demonstrate exceptional tactical aptitude and swimming ability.
* **Swimmer Canoeist Class**: Successful selection leads to the SC3 qualification course.
