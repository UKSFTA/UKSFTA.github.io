The Special Reconnaissance Regiment (SRR) conducts highly specialised surveillance and reconnaissance operations. Formed to meet the demand for a dedicated special reconnaissance capability, the SRR operates in diverse and high-threat environments.

## Core Roles
- **Covert Surveillance:** Detailed intelligence gathering through technical and human means.
- **Close Target Reconnaissance (CTR):** Providing detailed briefings on high-value objectives prior to intervention.
- **Technical Intelligence:** Utilising advanced systems to monitor and disrupt adversarial activity.
