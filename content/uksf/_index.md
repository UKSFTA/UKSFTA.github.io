E Squadron, colloquially known as **SF**, is a small, highly capable unit focused on surgical assaults and unconventional warfare. Operating under the directorate of Special Forces, the squadron executes sensitive taskings that require maximum precision and minimal footprint.

## Core Mandates

### Clandestine Direct Action
Executing raids and interdictions where operational security and deniability are paramount.

### HVT Acquisition
The identification, tracking, and neutralization of high-value targets in complex and hostile environments.

### Independent Tasking
SF elements often operate with minimal support, following complex briefs to dismantle adversary infrastructure ahead of the main combat element.
