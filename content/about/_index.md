## Institutional Overview

Welcome to **UKSF Taskforce Alpha (UKSFTA)**. We are a semi-realistic milsim community focused on simulating the Special Forces component of the United Kingdom Armed Forces. We conduct high-fidelity **Arma 3** missions and persistent campaigns, prioritizing technical aptitude, tactical coordination, and institutional integrity.

---

## Membership Lifecycle

Joining Taskforce Alpha is a structured process designed to ensure a mutual fit between the candidate and the taskforce.

### 01. Initial Application
Candidates must first review our **Rules and Regulations** and submit an entry packet. This initial handshake takes approximately 2 minutes and is the first step in our security clearance process.

### 02. Institutional Interview
Once a packet is reviewed by the Recruiting Team, the candidate is invited to our secure Discord node for an informal interview. This allows us to verify maturity and technical readiness.

### 03. Probationary Period (Ranger Regiment)
All new entrants are initially assigned as a **Rifleman** within the **Ranger Regiment**. This serves as a validation phase, allowing the recruit to master our core SOPs and ensure they integrate well with the existing team.

## Force Structure

Taskforce Alpha is comprised of three specialized operational elements, supported by aviation and medical detachments:

### 01. UK Ranger Regiment (Main Combat Element)
The primary fighting force of the taskforce. The Rangers are at the tip of the spear, engaging in major combat operations, air assaults, and mechanized infantry missions.
- **Direct Action**: High-intensity urban and rural combat.
- **Security**: Route clearance and area security protocols.
- **Entry**: The primary entry point for all new milsim players.

### 02. Special Forces Team (High-Skill Element)
A small, highly capable unit focused on surgical assaults, high-value target operations, and precision raids. 
- **Clandestine Ops**: Independent tasking behind enemy lines with minimal support.
- **Insertion**: Specialized covert insertions and extractions.
- **Status**: Selection-based with limited operational slots.

### 03. Intelligence & Sniper Team (ISR & Precision Support)
The intelligence backbone of the taskforce, providing Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (ISR).
- **Long-Range Recon**: Methodical information gathering and target tracking.
- **Precision Support**: Highly trained sniper/observer teams.
- **Mission Planning**: Collection and distribution of data for operational success.

### 04. Joint Special Forces Aviation Wing (JSFAW)
The air component of the taskforce, providing multi-domain aviation support including transport, CAS, and UAV overwatch.

### 05. Medical Detachment (RAMC)
Providing critical medical simulation support, including emergency field care and CASEVAC coordination.

## Operational Schedule

- **Main Operations**: Wednesdays at **19:00 GMT/BST**.
- **Unit Training**: Conducted dynamically based on team availability and role specialization.
- **Mini-Ops**: Periodic standalone deployments, often occurring on weekends to supplement the main campaign arc.

## The "Real Life First" Protocol

Taskforce Alpha operates on a **Real Life First** policy. We recognize that external commitments take priority. All we require is professional courtesy: notify your Chain of Command if you are unable to attend a scheduled operation.

**Institutional Attendance Standards:**
- **Inactive Status**: Initiated after 3 consecutive missed Main Operations (3 weeks) without notice. Re-application is required.
- **Discharge**: Members remaining unreachable for an additional 2 weeks (5 weeks total) will be removed from the unit registry.
