Medical support within the UKSF is provided by specialist RAMC personnel, including Patrol Medics and Medical Officers who have completed the gruelling UKSF selection process.

## Core Roles
- **Battlefield Advanced Trauma Life Support (BATLS):** High-tier clinical care at the point of injury.
- **Remote Clinical Support:** Maintaining the health and readiness of troops during long-duration covert missions.
- **Environmental Medicine:** Advising command on tropical, arctic, and desert health considerations.
