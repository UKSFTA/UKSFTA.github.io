The SFSG was formed to provide the SAS, SBS, and SRR with a dedicated infantry support capability. Comprised of 1st Battalion, The Parachute Regiment (1 PARA), and elements of the Royal Marines and RAF Regiment, the SFSG is a high-readiness force.

## Core Roles
- **Direct Action (DA):** Conducting raids and strikes alongside Tier 1 units.
- **Cordon and Containment:** Securing objective areas to allow specialized intervention.
- **Specialist Training:** Providing training and mentoring to partner forces globally.
