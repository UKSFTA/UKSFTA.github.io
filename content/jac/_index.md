Joint Aviation Command (JAC) coordinates the rotary and fixed-wing assets dedicated to special operations. This includes the specialist 750 (UKSF) Squadron and the 658 Squadron AAC.

## Core Roles
- **Infiltration & Exfiltration:** Covert delivery of personnel into high-threat environments.
- **Aerial Reconnaissance:** Providing real-time situational awareness for ground troops.
- **Casualty Evacuation (CASEVAC):** Rapid extraction of wounded personnel from the objective area.
