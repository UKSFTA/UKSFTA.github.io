The Army Special Operations Brigade (ASOB) is centered around the Ranger Regiment. While distinct from the Tier 1 SAS, the Rangers work in close proximity to the UKSF directorate to provide advisory and unconventional warfare support.

## Core Roles
- **Partner Force Engagement:** Training and operating alongside foreign militaries.
- **Unconventional Warfare:** Operating in complex political and physical environments.
- **Global Response:** Providing a permanent, high-readiness presence in key geographic regions.
