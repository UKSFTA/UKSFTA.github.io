The UK Ranger Regiment is the main combat element of Taskforce Alpha. While distinct from the Tier 1 Special Forces, the Rangers work in close proximity to provide high-intensity combat support and unconventional warfare capabilities.

## Operational Roles

### Direct Action
High-intensity urban and rural combat operations. Rangers are tasked with the primary assault mandate, seizing and holding key objectives.

### Air Assault & Mechanized
Rapid deployment via JSFAW rotary assets or mechanized infantry platforms. The Regiment maintains high mobility in all operational theaters.

### Security & Route Clearance
Securing supply lines and maintaining a safe operational environment for the wider taskgroup.

## Force Elements

### Infantry Sections
The primary tactical component of the Rangers. We currently maintain two fully operational infantry sections tasked with high-intensity combat and partner force engagement.

### Tactical Air Control Party (TACP)
Specialized personnel embedded within the Rangers to coordinate precision air support and joint fire effects with JSFAW assets.

### Armoured Detail
Tasked with the operation and maintenance of the taskforce's armored vehicle fleet, providing mobile protected firepower in hostile environments.

## Entry & Progression

The Ranger Regiment is the **Primary Entry Point** for all ground-based candidates.

* **Ranger Cadre**: All new recruits undergo the Ranger Cadre, mastering the fundamentals of special operations support.
* **Teamwork**: Ideal for players who enjoy working in larger squads and want consistent action.
