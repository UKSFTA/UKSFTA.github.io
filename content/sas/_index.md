The Special Air Service (SAS) remains the world's premier special forces unit. Tasked with high-readiness, unconventional warfare, and counter-terrorism operations, the Regiment operates in the shadows to maintain national security.

## Core Roles
- **Sabotage and Raiding:** Neutralising high-value targets behind enemy lines.
- **Counter-Terrorism (CT):** Domestic and international hostage rescue and intervention.
- **Special Reconnaissance:** Covert intelligence gathering in denied environments.
