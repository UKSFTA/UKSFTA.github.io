Taskforce Headquarters (TFHQ) is the central command node for our milsim community. It coordinates weekly operations, mission design, and technical infrastructure.

## Core Operational Domains

### Intelligence Cell (Zeus)
Our dedicated mission makers and Zeus operators. They are responsible for crafting immersive, high-standard campaigns and managing real-time operational flow.

### QM Department
The Quartermasters Department handles modpack management, technical logistics, and ensuring all community assets are mission-ready.

## Entry & Progression

Roles within TFHQ are primarily **Appointment Only**.

* **Zeus/Mission Makers**: Members who have proven their dedication and creativity within the unit.
* **QM Team**: Technical specialists responsible for the community's digital infrastructure.
