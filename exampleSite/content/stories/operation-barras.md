OFFICIAL SENSITIVE: Joint After-Action Report.
Subject: Hostage Recovery / Militia Neutralization.
Location: Gberi Bana / Magbeni, Sierra Leone.
Assets: 22 SAS, SBS, SFSG, JSFAW.
{{< /mandate >}}

## 1. Situation
On 25 August 2000, 11 members of the Royal Irish Regiment and their Sierra Leonean liaison officer were captured by the 'West Side Boys' militia. Following failed negotiations, a joint-service kinetic rescue was authorized.

- **Adversary**: West Side Boys (approx. 400+ irregulars).
- **Environment**: Riparian jungle, non-permissive terrain across the Rokel Creek.
- **ROE**: Decisive kinetic force authorized for hostage protection and militia degradation.

## 2. Mission
To conduct a simultaneous, heli-borne assault on two separate militia strongholds (Gberi Bana and Magbeni) to recover all captives and achieve total neutralization of the militia presence.

## 3. Execution (CONOPS)
- **Phase 1: Insertion**: Three Chinook HC2s and one Lynx AH7 (JSFAW) conducted a low-level, high-speed approach to Rokel Creek to achieve acoustic surprise.
- **Phase 2: Assault**: D Squadron (22 SAS) fast-roped into Gberi Bana to secure the hostages. Simultaneously, SBS teams conducted a littoral approach to secure river transit routes.
- **Phase 3: Cordon & Support**: A reinforced company of 1 PARA (SFSG) assaulted Magbeni to neutralize militia heavy weapons and prevent external reinforcement of the hostage node.

## 4. Mission Assessment
- **Asset Status**: 11/11 hostages recovered.
- **Operational Effect**: West Side Boys effectively neutralized as a regional threat.
- **Casualties**: 1 Tier 1 operator (Sgt Bradley Tinnion) KIA; 12 WIA.
