## Tactical Medicine

TFA Medical Specialists provide high-standard clinical care within our simulation environment. Beyond standard combat medicine, you are trained in advanced trauma management using our community's medical mods and SOPs.

### Core Competencies

- **Field Surgery**: Battlefield stabilization and emergency surgical intervention using simulated medical systems.
- **CASEVAC**: Coordination of casualty evacuation from contested zones.
- **Medical Training**: Providing instruction to other unit members on basic life-saving protocols.

### Mission Profile

As a medic within Taskforce Alpha, you are an operator first. You will be expected to maintain combat effectiveness while delivering life-saving care during our weekly operations.
