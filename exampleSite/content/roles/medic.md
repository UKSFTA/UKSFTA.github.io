## Tactical Medicine

UKSF Medical Assistants provide world-class clinical care in the most demanding environments on earth. Beyond standard combat medicine, you are trained in advanced trauma management and long-term clinical care in isolated theatres.

### Core Competencies

- **Trauma Surgery**: Battlefield stabilization and emergency surgical intervention.
- **Pharmacology**: Management of restricted pharmaceutical assets in the field.
- **Remote Diagnostics**: Utilizing advanced biometrics to monitor operator health in real-time.

### Mission Profile

As a medic within the Joint Special Forces, you are an operator first. You will be expected to insert via HALO/HAHO or maritime platforms and maintain combat effectiveness while delivering life-saving care.
