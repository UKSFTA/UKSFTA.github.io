The Ranger Regiment is a specialized unit of the British Army, part of the Army Special Operations Brigade. Within UKSF Taskforce Alpha, the Rangers provide a persistent, lighter-footprint capability designed to operate in high-threat environments globally.

## Core Operational Domains

### Partner Force Integration
Training, advising, and leading partner forces during unconventional warfare and counter-insurgency operations.

### Asymmetric Strike
Conducting precision offensive operations and tactical sabotage in support of strategic objectives.

## Entry & Progression

The Ranger Regiment is an advanced specialization within UKSFTA.

* **Requirements**: Members typically spend an introductory period in SFSG before specializing.
* **Selection**: Focuses on individuals with high intelligence, cultural empathy, and the ability to operate effectively within a partner force structure.
