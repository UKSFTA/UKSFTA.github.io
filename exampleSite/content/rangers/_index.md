The Ranger Regiment is a special operations unit of the British Army, established in 2021 as a core component of the Army Special Operations Brigade. It provides the Directorate with a persistent, lighter-footprint capability designed to operate embedded with foreign forces in high-threat environments globally.

## Strategic Role & The Grey Zone

The Rangers are the UK's specialists in **Capacity Building**â€”working alongside partner nation forces to enhance their effectiveness against strategic threats. They are designed to operate in the "Grey Zone," where influence and training are as critical as kinetic effect.

### Core Operational Domains

*   **Partner Force Integration**: Training, advising, and leading international partner forces during unconventional warfare and counter-insurgency operations.
*   **Asymmetric Strike**: Conducting precision offensive operations and tactical sabotage in support of strategic objectives, often while embedded with local units.
*   **Special Reconnaissance**: Gathering intelligence in complex, human-centric environments where a Tier 1 footprint would be counter-productive to long-term stability.
*   **Strategic Influence**: Projecting British military influence through long-term regional persistence.

## Regimental Structure

The Regiment consists of four battalions, each regionally aligned to deep cultural and linguistic mastery:

*   **1 RANGERS**: Regionally aligned to West Africa.
*   **2 RANGERS**: Regionally aligned to East Africa.
*   **3 RANGERS**: Regionally aligned to Europe and the Mediterranean.
*   **4 RANGERS**: Regionally aligned to the Middle East and South Asia.

## Selection & Assessment

<div id="selection"></div>

Ranger Selection is designed to find individuals who possess high intelligence, cultural empathy, and the ability to operate effectively without a standard command structure.

### The Selection Phases:
- **Ranger Assessment Course**: A rigorous 2-week course testing physical endurance, tactical proficiency, and instructional ability.
- **Special Operations Course**: 10 weeks of advanced training in asymmetric tactics, language skills, and partner-force integration.
- **Regionally Aligned Training**: Specialized training focused on the culture, geography, and language of the operator's assigned theatre of operations.
