18 (UKSF) Signal Regiment provides dedicated signals intelligence (SIGINT), electronic warfare (EW), and secure communications support to the Taskforce. Our members are technical specialists who bridge the gap between intelligence and kinetic action.

## Technical Capabilities

### Signals Intelligence (SIGINT)
Intercepting and decrypting adversary command networks. We provide the electronic signature required to locate high-value targets and disrupt enemy coordination.

### Electronic Warfare (EW)
Dominating the electromagnetic spectrum. Using specialized tools to jam enemy frequencies, protect friendly assets, and exploit technical vulnerabilities in the battlespace.

## Entry & Progression

Signals is a technical specialization within Taskforce Alpha. Candidates must demonstrate high technical aptitude and a commitment to meticulous data gathering.

* **Eligibility**: Open to members who have completed their initial orientation and show interest in technical playstyles.
* **Training**: Intensive training on radio protocols, mapping tools, and signals equipment.
* **Specialization**: Pathways into cyber operations or forward SIGINT detachments.
