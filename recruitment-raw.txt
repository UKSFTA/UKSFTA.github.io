We are recruiting all skill levels. New players start as Cadets to receive high-quality training before specializing.

Initial Entry:
* SFSG (Special Forces Support Group): The required entry point for all new recruits to learn SOPs and tactics.

Specialist Roles (Internal Transfer Required):
* SAS (Assault Element): Primary door-kickers. Specialists in CQB and Direct Action.

* SBS (Maritime/Support): Experts in EOD and amphibious insertion/extraction, operating alongside SAS.

* SRR (Intelligence/Recce): Provides deep observation and critical ELINT/ISR. SRR is strictly internal transfer only. Requires proving excellence in SAS/SBS and approval from Command.

* JSFAW (Aviation Wing): Dedicated pilots for fast-rope, CAS, and transport. URGENT need for experienced pilots.

* Intelligence Cell (Zeus): Dedicated mission makers for weekly operations.
